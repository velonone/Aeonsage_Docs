# AeonSage

<Frame>
  ![AEO Nofficial Logo03](/images/AEONofficial_logo03.svg)
</Frame>

**Build intelligent agents that respect your sovereignty.**

AeonSage is a self-hosted AI orchestration platform that connects language models, messaging channels, and real-world tools—giving you complete control over your digital intelligence infrastructure.

---

## Why AeonSage?

| Feature               | Description                                                                                          |
| --------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Sovereignty First** | Your data, your infrastructure, your rules. Run entirely on-premise with zero external dependencies. |
| **Omni-Channel**      | Reach your agents through Telegram, Discord, WhatsApp, Slack, and 20+ more platforms—all unified.    |
| **Model Agnostic**    | Use GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, or local models via Ollama. Switch providers without changing code.       |
| **Production Ready**  | Built for scale with enterprise-grade security, monitoring, and reliability.                         |
